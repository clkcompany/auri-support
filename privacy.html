Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

The short version: Auri does not collect, store or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Auri app ("Auri", "the app"), published by CLK Company ("we", "us"), handles information.

Data we collect

None. Auri has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs and no tracking of any kind. We cannot see who you are, which themes you use, or what you put in your widgets.

Your photos

When you add a photo to a widget, Auri uses Apple's system photo picker. The photo you choose is processed and stored only on your device, inside the app's private storage shared with its widget extension. Your photos are never uploaded anywhere.

Your texts and dates

Captions, quotes, city names, event names, countdown and relationship dates are stored locally on your device. They never leave it.

Weather

If you use a weather widget, Auri sends the coordinates of the city you typed to the free Open-Meteo service to fetch a forecast. That request contains only latitude and longitude — no identifiers, no account information, nothing personal. Auri does not request or use your device's GPS location.

Purchases

Subscriptions and one-time purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Auri never sees or stores your payment details. See Apple's Privacy Policy for how Apple handles purchase data.

Children

Auri does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Your rights

Because we hold no data about you, there is nothing for us to export, correct or delete. Removing the app from your device removes everything Auri stored.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page and the effective date above. Since Auri collects no data, changes are unlikely.
