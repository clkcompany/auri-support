Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

NFC Tap is built to respect your privacy. We do not run our own servers, we do not have user accounts, and we do not collect or sell your personal data. This policy explains exactly what the app does with information.

In short: Everything you create in NFC Tap stays on your device. We have no analytics, no tracking, and no ad networks. We never see your tags, notes, or PINs.

Information we collect

None on our side. NFC Tap does not send your personal data to us or to any third-party analytics or advertising service. There is no sign-up and no account.

Data stored on your device

  • Scan & write history and saved task profiles are stored locally on your iPhone only.
  • Secret Tags: private notes are encrypted with a PIN you choose and written to your NFC tag. The PIN is never stored anywhere and never leaves your device. If you forget it, the note cannot be recovered.
  • The contents you read from or write to NFC tags are processed on your device and are not transmitted to us.

Network connections

NFC Tap works offline for its core features. Two optional features make network requests:

  • Map location picker: loads map tiles from OpenStreetMap. Your IP address is visible to that service when tiles load, per their standard operation. See the OpenStreetMap privacy policy.
  • Opening links or apps you write to a tag uses your device's normal browser or App Store, governed by their own policies.

Purchases

Subscriptions and the lifetime unlock are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment details. Apple's handling of that data is covered by the Apple Privacy Policy.

Permissions

NFC Tap requests access to NFC to read and write tags, only while you are actively scanning. It does not access your contacts, photos, or location in the background.

Children

NFC Tap is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Deleting your data

You can clear your history and saved profiles anytime in the app under Settings. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data from your device.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
